# Codeforces 1999A - A+B Again?

## Problem Link

https://codeforces.com/problemset/problem/1999/A

---

## Idea

Find the sum of the digits of `n`.

first = n / 10
last = n % 10

---

## Formula

answer = (n / 10) + (n % 10)


---

## Complexity

- Time Complexity: `O(1)`
- Space Complexity: `O(1)`
